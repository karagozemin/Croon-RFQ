03 — THE CRON JOB FUNERAL

CRON JOB †

Re-runs the same command.

Hard-wired to one provider.

No price discipline.

Stateless. Forgets everything.

Crashes on provider failure.

No proof of work or spend.

CROON RFQ

Re-opens a market, every run.

Competitively selects among agents.

Hard budget rule — over-budget bids die.

Stateful: budgets, spend, full history.

Budget-protecting fallback ladder.

Signed receipt with on-chain tx hash.

The floor is open.
